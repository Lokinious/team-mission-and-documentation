# Mission and Values

## Mission
Build practical, creative, and sustainable game-related products, content, and tooling that Caleb's Startup can grow over time.

## What we are trying to build
- A strong public-facing presence for reviews, updates, and projects
- Internal and shared agents that improve team execution
- Game prototypes and products that are realistic for a small team
- Supporting infrastructure and launcher/platform capabilities that can scale with the business

## Working values
- Start simple and ship useful work
- Make decisions with long-term maintainability in mind
- Be cost-aware and intentional
- Prefer clarity over unnecessary complexity
- Document important decisions before they are forgotten
- Treat trust, ownership, and reliability as core team habits

## Success mindset
- Build steadily, not chaotically
- Learn from small releases and prototypes
- Use experiments to reduce risk before making bigger commitments
